National Parks In Asia

We are here to help you discover, explore, and learn more about the national parks in Asia and why we need to protect them.




Ancient Goreme National Park

Goreme National Park features one of the most unique attractions in Turkey combining nature and culture.

Asia / Turkey



Bukhansan National Park

The Bukhansanseong Fortress and Baekundae, the park's tallest peak, are pinnacle attractions within Bukhansan National Park.

Asia / South Korea



Fuji-Hakone-Izu National Park

Fuji-Hakone-Izu National Park is home to Mount Fuji and the Fuji Five Lakes. It is the most popular tourist destination in Japan.

Asia / Japan




Nikko National Park

Nikko National Park delivers magnificent shrines and temples intertwined in natural landscapes making it a Japan favorite.

Asia / Japan



Seoraksan National Park

Seoraksan National Park of South Korean combines cultural Buddhist temples with stunning waterfalls and mountainous landscapes.

Asia / South Korea



Shiretoko National Park

Shiretoko National Park of Japan represents true wilderness and the place for adventurous hikers who want to climb Mount Rausu.

Asia / Japan




Yala National Park

Yala National Park is the most visited national park in Sri Lanka and home to the densest population of leopard in the world.

Asia / Sri Lanka



Hallasan National Park

Hallasan National Park on Jeju Island features the tallest mountain in South Korea making it one of the most popular destinations.

Asia / South Korea



Jigme Dorji National Park

Jigme Dorji National Park in Bhutan is home to some of the most renowned Himalayan trekking in the world.

Asia / Bhutan




Naejangsan National Park

Naejangsan National Park delivers one of the most stunning displays of color during the fall foliage creating a popular place for locals in South Korea.

Asia / South Korea



Phnom Kulen National Park

Phnom Kulen National Park in Cambodia intertwines the cultural and historical features of the park into beautiful natural landscapes.

Asia / Cambodia



Royal Manas National Park

Royal Manas National Park is the oldest protected area in Bhutan and the Conservation Showpiece of the Kingdom.

Asia / Bhutan




Songnisan National Park

Songnisan National Park delivers both natural and cultural highlights that draw both international and local visitors to this South Korean treasure.

Asia / South Korea



Jirisan National Park

Jirisan National Park was the first national park created in South Korea and is the largest terrestrial national park in the country.

Asia / South Korea



National parks are set aside for public recreation and enjoyment or for its historical or scientific interest while keeping most landscapes and their accompanying plants and animals in their natural state
