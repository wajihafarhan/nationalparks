National Parks In Europe

We are here to help you discover, explore, and learn more about the national parks in Europe and why we need to protect them.




Cinque Terre National Park

Although the smallest national park in Italy, Cinque Terre National Park, is one of the most culturally relevant.

Europe / Italy



Dolomiti Bellunesi National Park

Dolomiti Bellunesi National Park is home to the Dolomites which are some of the most renowned and beautiful mountains in the world.

Europe / Italy



Killarney National Park

Bogs, cliffs, crags, heath, lakes, moorland, mountains, rivers, and rock formations make Killarney National Park a special place.

Europe / Ireland




Ordesa & Monte Perdido National Park

Ordesa & Monte Perdido National Park is the protected area of the Pyrenees in Spain with Mont Perdido reaching a peak of 11,000 feet.

Europe / Spain



Prielbrusye National Park

Mount Elbrus is the tallest mountain in Europe and the pinnacle attraction for Prielbrusye National Park.

Europe / Russia / Kabardino-Balkaria



Rodna Mountains National Park

Rodna National Park features the longest mountain range in Romania and delivers caves, crevasses, and valleys to park visitors.

Europe / Romania




Saxon Switzerland National Park

Saxon Switzerland National Park in Germany is home to the Elbe Sandstone Mountains full of stunning rock formations and forests.

Europe / Germany



Connemara National Park

Connemara National Park in Ireland displays the majestic Twelve Bens mountains while celebrating the glory of Kylemore Abbey.

Europe / Ireland



Domogled-Valea Cernei National Park

Domogled Valea Cernei National Park features waterfalls, mountains, caves, sinkholes, & lakes that highlight the beauty of Romania.

Europe / Romania




Losiny Ostrov National Park

Losiny Ostrov National Park in Russia is pristine wilderness with parts of it actually located in the metropolitan area of Moscow. Come explore.

Europe / Russia / Moscow Oblast



Retezat National Park

Retezat National Park displays over 80 glacial lakes across some of the largest massifs and most extensive old-growth forest in Romania.

Europe / Romania



Bavarian Forest National Park

Bavarian Forest National Park was the first in Germany and it works with a neighboring park to create the largest protected forest in Europe.

Europe / Germany




Vodlozersky National Park

Vodlozersky National Park delivers a pristine wilderness blanketed with forested hills that surround several lakes creating one of Russia's most amazing parks.

Europe / Russia / Arkhangelsk Oblast



Wicklow Mountains National Park

Wicklow Mountains National Park celebrates a combination of historical, cultural, and natural gems of Ireland. Come Explore.

Europe / Ireland



National parks are set aside for public recreation and enjoyment or for its historical or scientific interest while keeping most landscapes and their accompanying plants and animals in their natural state
