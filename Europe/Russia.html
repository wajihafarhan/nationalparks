National Parks In Russia

We are here to help you discover, explore, and learn more about the national parks in Russia and why we need to protect them.




Prielbrusye National Park

Mount Elbrus is the tallest mountain in Europe and the pinnacle attraction for Prielbrusye National Park.

Europe / Russia / Kabardino-Balkaria



Losiny Ostrov National Park

Losiny Ostrov National Park in Russia is pristine wilderness with parts of it actually located in the metropolitan area of Moscow. Come explore.

Europe / Russia / Moscow Oblast



Vodlozersky National Park

Vodlozersky National Park delivers a pristine wilderness blanketed with forested hills that surround several lakes creating one of Russia's most amazing parks.
