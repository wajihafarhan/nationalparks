National Parks In Africa

We are here to help you discover, explore, and learn more about the national parks in Africa and why we need to protect them.




Chobe National Park

Chobe National Park in Botswana is an elephant capital of Africa having over 50,000 elephants visible from land and the river.

Africa / Botswana



Kakum National Park

The forest elephants are one of the special treats of Kakum National Park. The park is Ghana's leading nature attraction.

Africa / Ghana



Mana Pools National Park

Mana Pools National Park in Zimbabwe is one of the best safari destinations during the dry season where animals come to drink.

Africa / Zimbabwe




Ranomafana National Park

Ranomafana National Park is one of the most visited destinations in Madagascar with about 20 species of lemurs.

Africa / Madagascar



Ras Muhammad National Park

Located on the Sinai Peninsula, Ras Muhammad National Park protects the start of the Red Sea Reef.

Africa / Egypt



Simien Mountains National Park

Simien Mountains National Park is one of the leading attractions for Ethiopia providing visitors with views of abundant wildlife.

Africa / Ethiopia





Tassili n’Ajjer National Park

Algeria's Tassili n’Ajjer National Park is home to one of the greatest collections of prehistoric cave art in the world.

Africa / Algeria



Victoria Falls National Park

Victoria Falls National Park is home to the largest waterfall in the world and one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World.

Africa / Zimbabwe



Virunga National Park

Virunga National Park is one of the best places to experience the mountain gorillas. The national park is one of the first in Africa.

Africa / Congo, DR




Waza National Park

Waza National Park features abundant wildlife and guided safaris to experience the wilderness and wildlife of Cameroon.

Africa / Cameroon



Bale Mountains National Park

Bale Mountains National Park in Ethiopia is home to one of the greatest percentages of endemic species in the world.

Africa / Ethiopia



Korup National Park

The rainforest in Korup National Park in Cameroon represents one of the most diverse ecosystems in all of Africa.

Africa / Cameroon




Mangroves National Park

Mangroves National Park in DR Congo is the only marine-oriented national park in the country while protecting endangered manatee.

Africa / Congo, DR



Masoala National Park

Masoala National Park is one of the most diverse national parks in Madagascar creating a coveted attraction for nature lovers.

Africa / Madagascar



Mole National Park

Mole National Park in Ghana is the country's first and largest national park and some argue the most prestigious protected area as well.

Africa / Ghana




Wadi El Gemal National Park

Wadi El Gemal National Park in Egypt protects a portion of the Red Sea reef and its 1,200 species of fish and 450 species of coral.

Africa / Egypt



White Desert National Park

White Desert National Park in Egypt protects the splendor of the White Desert and the diversity of the Red Sea Reef.

Africa / Egypt



National parks are set aside for public recreation and enjoyment or for its historical or scientific interest while keeping most landscapes and their accompanying plants and animals in their natural state
